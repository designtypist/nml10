Event Overview

Description

No Mans LAN is an gaming event hosted every year at St.Clair College by the ITC Club. Players show up with their computers, connect into a network designed by the ITC Club and play in tournaments. Other gaming events are available during the weekend long event. The No Mans LAN is not just for the competitive top tier gamer. Anyone can come out. Being able to enjoy the games you like with hundreds of other like-minded players is an experience you will never forget. This year the LAN party is expected to be even bigger. This year marks the 10th anniversary of No Mans LAN and we’re pulling out all the stops this time; bigger prizes, bigger venue, more events and a much faster network.

Tournament Rules

All these rules applies to all tournament games

  • Players must prepared to play 15 minutes before the start of their match.
  • Tournament supervisors have the authority to disapprove equipment brought by players.
  • Players are not allowed to use add-ons provided by a third party.
  • If the Tournament matches start without the permission from a supervisor, the match is restarted.
  • Tournament supervisors may issue a time limit for the player’s absence. (For example: a bathroom break)
  • After every match, a maximum of a 5 minute break will be allowed.
  • Players must act politely and in good manner towards both supervisors and fellow competitors.
  • Ultimately the tournament supervisors have the right to change the rules if needed.
  • In conflict situations tournament supervisors have the highest authority, less God himself.
  • Players must be registered for the Tournament prior to the start of the tournament in order to participate.
  • In case of technical failure or connection loss during a game, inform a tournament supervisor immediately.
  • All activity that indicates cheating may lead to losing the match and removal from the tournament as well as from the event.
  • After the game has ended, both players are obligated of informing the result to tournament supervisors.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring?
Please remember to bring a Power Bar. Also optional: Energy Drinks, Food, Your PC (This is a BYOC event, Bring your own computer.)
Will there be food?
Yes, we will have chips and pop for sale during the event. Also, We will be bringing in PIZZA, (gamer food) available for everyone, free of charge on Saturday night
Is there a minumum age?
It's an ALL ages event, anybody may join. Although, anybody under the age of 13 should be accompanied by an adult.
What if I can't find a team?
Team registration will take place before tournament starts (AT THE EVENT). You can find a team during the event on our website forums.
How do I register my team?
Team registration will take place before tournament starts. This will be done on our website "DURING" the event.
